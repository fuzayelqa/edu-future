STEM Initiative

Bridging the Digital Divide

Our "Code for Tomorrow" initiative launched in 5 new districts this year. By providing solar-powered computer labs and dedicated coding instructors, we've enabled rural students to access the global digital economy.

  • 25 New Computer Labs
  • Python & JS Curriculum
